200 - Multiplication

Time Limit:1 sec Memory Limit: 32 MB


You are a great coder .so it is an easy task for you.Take two numbers then print their multiplication.


Input

At first an integer T (1<=T<=100) that means number of testcase. Then 2 integers a and b in a single line.
0<= a,b <=1000

Output

You have to print the multiplication of two numbers


Sample Input

5
1 1
2 3
10 5
21 34
56 6

Sample Output

1
6
50
714
336
